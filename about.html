OUR STORY

A Pause by Kalawewa

Nestled in the calm beauty of Kalawewa, Tea Break is more than just a place to eat — it’s a pause from the rush of everyday life.

We created Tea Break for people who love good food, good tea, and good vibes. From freshly brewed Sri Lankan tea to comforting short eats and café-style favorites, every item is prepared with care, flavor, and a touch of home.

Whether you’re stopping by for a quick bite, catching up with friends, or just enjoying a quiet moment with a cup of tea, Tea Break is your perfect escape.
